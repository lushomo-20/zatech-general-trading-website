About Zatech General Trading Limited

Zatech General Trading Limited is a dynamic and versatile company offering a wide range of products and services. Established with a commitment to excellence, we serve construction, agriculture, and automotive industries with high-quality products and solutions tailored to diverse client needs.

Our presence extends throughout the Copperbelt, with our head office located in Kitwe and plans to expand to other areas of the country. The company is built on a strong team of professionals with proven track records and affiliations to relevant professional bodies.

Vision: To be the world's premier merchants delivering products and services to a global marketplace. Our motto is "Putting you first", ensuring customers come first through quality products and service that help them succeed.
